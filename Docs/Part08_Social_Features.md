# OPENLOBBY MASTER AI SPECIFICATION

## Part 8 — Social Features

Version: V2.0

### 1. POSTS & FEEDS

Posts are the core content unit. They support text and images.
Feeds are chronological and use cursor-based pagination for infinite scrolling.

### 2. INTERACTIONS

Users can like, comment, and bookmark posts.
Comments support nesting for replies.

### 3. NOTIFICATIONS

Triggered for likes, comments, replies, and mentions.
Aggregated to prevent spam.

### 4. MESSAGES

Direct messaging supports real-time updates and read receipts.
